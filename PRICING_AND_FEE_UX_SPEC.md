## KHI NÀO USER THẤY FEE

| Screen      | Display              | Note                   |
| ----------- | -------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Deal detail | Total = amount + fee | Only when deal created |
| Receipt     | Breakdown            | After release          |
| Proposal    | Amount only          | Fee shown on accept    |

---

## PLACEMENT TRONG APP

- Deal detail: "Deal total: $X (includes Y% fee)"
- Receipt: "Amount: $A | Platform fee: $B | Total: $C"
- Proposal: No fee shown until accept

---

## TRANSPARENCY RULES

1. Always show fee before commitment
2. Always show breakdown on receipt
3. Never surprise with hidden fees

---

## COPYWRITING RULES

- Avoid: "Fee"
- Use: "Platform contribution" or "Service fee"
- Avoid: "Charged"
- Use: "Applied" or "Included"
