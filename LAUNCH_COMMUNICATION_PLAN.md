## FAQ LAUNCH COPY

### What is SkillValue AI?

AI-powered marketplace connecting freelancers with projects. Uses AI for matching, trust scores for transparency.

### Is it free?

Yes, 1.0 launch is free to use. No fees at launch.

### How does trust work?

Users earn trust scores based on completed deals and reviews. Both sides rate each other after.

### How does AI matching work?

When you post a request or offer, AI finds the best matches based on skills, price, history.

### Is my money safe?

We use a deal protection model. Payment is held until work is approved.

### Can I use for my business?

Yes! Post projects, receive proposals, hire - all in the app.

### How do you handle disputes?

Our team reviews disputes. Both sides get heard. Resolution within 48 hours.
