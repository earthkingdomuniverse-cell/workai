# WorkAI Marketplace Liquidity Plan

## Purpose

Practical liquidity plan for building a healthy two-sided WorkAI marketplace.

## Liquidity challenge

A marketplace needs both supply and demand at the same time. If there are too few offers, requests do not convert. If there are too few requests, providers leave.

## Minimum viable marketplace targets

| Metric | Target | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Active offers | 50+ unique providers | Enough supply to browse |
| Active requests | 20+ unique requesters | Enough demand to match |
| Complete provider profiles | 80%+ | Trust and quality |
| Complete requester profiles | 80%+ | Trust and quality |
| Proposals per request | 2+ | Marketplace competition |

## Healthy marketplace targets

| Metric | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Active offers | 200+ |
| Active requests | 100+ |
| Matches per week | 50+ |
| Deals per week | 10+ |

## Launch category focus

| Category | Reason | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Design | Clear deliverables and strong demand | Wedge |
| Video editing | High demand and clear output | High |
| Content writing | Low friction and easy to scope | High |
| Web development | Higher value, more scope complexity | Medium |
| Marketing | Broad and harder to define | Later |

## Categories to avoid at launch

- Legal services
- Highly subjective consulting
- Categories requiring heavy compliance or specialist moderation

## Supply strategy

| Source | Method | Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Personal network | Invite freelancers and builders | 20 providers |
| Community groups | Post useful invite in relevant groups | 30 providers |
| Freelance platforms | Manual outreach to quality providers | 20 providers |
| Referral | Existing users invite providers | 20 providers |

### Offer quality threshold

- Clear title
- Defined price or range
- Description long enough to understand scope
- At least one skill tag
- Profile quality threshold met

## Demand strategy

| Source | Method | Target |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Personal network | Startup founders, agency owners, operators | 15 requesters |
| LinkedIn | Direct outreach to small businesses | 15 requesters |
| Founder communities | Manual request sourcing | 10 requesters |
| Referrals | Providers invite clients/requesters | 10 requesters |

### Request quality threshold

- Specific title
- Budget range or clear willingness to pay
- Description with context
- Skills needed
- Deadline/urgency if relevant

## AI match for cold start

AI helps prevent ghost marketplace behavior by pushing relevant supply and demand to each side.

### AI workflow

1. User posts request.
2. AI suggests matching offers/providers.
3. Provider sees matching request.
4. Requester receives provider suggestions.
5. Operator can manually intervene when match quality is weak.

## Ghost marketplace warning signs

| Signal | Risk | Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Proposals per offer below 0.5 | Demand weak | Recruit requesters |
| Proposals per request below 1 | Supply weak or match weak | Recruit providers / improve matching |
| More than 50% offers with zero views | Discoverability issue | Improve search and recommendations |
| More than 70% requests with zero proposals | Match quality issue | Manual matching and AI tuning |

## Trade-offs

| Decision | Trade-off | Accepted |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Focus on design first | Slower category expansion | Yes |
| Manual recruitment | Not organic initially | Yes |
| AI matching with low data | Less accurate early | Yes |
| Quality over quantity | Slower supply growth | Yes |
