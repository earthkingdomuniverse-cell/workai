# WorkAI Revenue Streams Map

## Purpose

Map possible WorkAI revenue streams for launch, post-launch, and later-stage phases.

## Launch revenue streams: 1.0

None by default.

WorkAI 1.0 prioritizes liquidity, trust, completion, and learning over immediate monetization.

## Post-launch revenue streams: 1.x

### Stream 1: Platform fee / take rate

| Attribute | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Who pays | Provider, requester, or split depending on testing |
| Why they pay | Matching, trust, deal workflow, dispute support |
| Conversion impact | Medium |
| Trust impact | Low if transparent |
| Complexity | Medium |
| Launch when | Phase 1.2 or after deal flow proves reliable |

### Stream 2: Premium visibility

| Attribute | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Who pays | Provider wanting more exposure |
| Why they pay | More views and proposal opportunities |
| Conversion impact | Low if optional |
| Trust impact | Must be labeled clearly as promoted |
| Complexity | Low |
| Launch when | Phase 1.1+ |

### Stream 3: Pro account

| Attribute | Detail |
| --- | --- |
| Who pays | Power users |
| Why they pay | Analytics, workflow tools, AI support, advanced visibility |
| Conversion impact | Medium if badly gated |
| Trust impact | Medium/high depending on feature design |
| Complexity | High |
| Launch when | Phase 1.x |

## Experimental streams

| Stream | Description | Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| AI copilot subscription | Advanced AI matching/pricing/support/next-action tools | Research |
| Operator tools | Advanced admin/risk/fraud tooling | Later |
| Enterprise | Custom work marketplace or talent operations solution | Later |

## Guardrails

- Do not monetize before first value.
- Do not sell trust.
- Do not hide paid placement.
- Do not make reviews pay-to-play.
- Do not gate basic marketplace liquidity.
