# WorkAI Enterprise and Operator Monetization

## Purpose

Define later-stage enterprise and operator monetization angles for WorkAI without pulling them into the 1.0 launch too early.

## Later-stage B2B use cases

| Use case | Description | Value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Talent operations | Companies source and manage skilled providers | High |
| Agency management | Agencies manage contractors and deal workflows | High |
| Internal skill marketplace | Companies match internal needs to internal/external skills | High |
| Operator tools | Advanced risk, dispute, and trust operations tooling | Medium/high |

## Why not launch immediately

- WorkAI needs marketplace liquidity first.
- Core member/provider/requester flows need stronger proof.
- Enterprise sales cycle can distract from 1.0 activation.
- B2B value depends on reliable trust, deal, and analytics data.

## What to instrument now

1. Deal completion volume.
2. Category demand and supply distribution.
3. User quality metrics.
4. Trust score movement.
5. Dispute and fraud trends.
6. Repeat requester/provider behavior.
7. Team or agency-like usage patterns.

## Future monetization paths

- Enterprise dashboard.
- Contractor or vendor management workspace.
- Managed marketplace for teams.
- AI-powered talent matching API.
- Operator/risk tooling subscription.
