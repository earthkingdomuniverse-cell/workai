# WorkAI Landing Page Copy

## Purpose

Landing page messaging for WorkAI 1.0.

## Hero

### Headline

Find the right work help faster with AI.

### Subheadline

WorkAI is an AI-powered work marketplace that connects people with skills to people who need help, using matching, trust scores, pricing guidance, and structured deal workflows.

### Primary CTA

Get Started Free

### Secondary CTA

Explore How WorkAI Works

## Value propositions

### AI matching

WorkAI matches requests, offers, providers, and clients based on skills, scope, budget, trust, and work history.

### Trust scores

Users build trust through completed deals, reviews, verification, and platform behavior.

### Simple deals

Move from request to proposal to deal to review in one structured workflow.

## Feature bullets

- Post your skills or your work needs.
- Get AI-powered recommendations.
- Use pricing guidance before quoting or funding.
- Review trust signals before working together.
- Manage proposals and deals in one place.
- Build reputation through completed work.

## Trust and safety section

WorkAI is designed around trust:

- Visible trust signals
- Reviews from completed deals
- Dispute handling
- Operator/admin review for risky cases
- Fraud and risk monitoring foundation

## AI section

AI helps users:

- Find better matches
- Suggest fair prices
- Recommend next actions
- Classify support issues
- Surface risky or disputed activity for operators

## Onboarding expectations

### First 5 minutes

- Create account.
- Select role.
- Add profile and skills.
- Choose goals.

### First session

- See AI next action.
- Create first offer or request.
- Use AI pricing or matching guidance.

### First week

- Send or receive proposal.
- Create first deal.
- Complete first review where possible.
