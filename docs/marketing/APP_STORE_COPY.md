# WorkAI App Store Copy

## Purpose

Draft app store listing copy for WorkAI on iOS and Android.

## App Store: iOS

### Title

WorkAI

### Subtitle

AI-Powered Work Marketplace

### Promotional text

Find help and work with AI

### Description

WorkAI is an AI-powered work marketplace that helps people find the right help, projects, providers, and opportunities faster.

From design to development to writing to business support, WorkAI uses AI matching, trust signals, fair pricing guidance, and structured deal workflows to reduce search friction and improve confidence.

## Why WorkAI

### Smart AI matching

WorkAI matches requests, offers, providers, and clients based on skills, price, fit, trust, and work history.

### Trust scores

Users build trust through completed deals, reviews, verification, and marketplace behavior.

### Fair pricing guidance

AI helps providers quote with confidence and helps clients understand reasonable budget ranges.

### Simple deal flow

Move from request to proposal to deal to review in one place.

## For providers

- Get discovered through AI matching
- Build trust through completed deals
- Find requests that match your skills
- Use AI pricing guidance

## For clients/requesters

- Find help from relevant providers
- Use trust signals before hiring
- Compare proposals in one place
- Move from request to deal with less friction

## How it works

1. Create your profile and add your skills.
2. Post an offer or request.
3. Get matched with relevant people or work.
4. Send or receive proposals.
5. Complete deals and build trust.
