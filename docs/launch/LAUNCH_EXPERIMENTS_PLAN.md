# WorkAI Launch Experiments Plan

## Purpose

Experiment backlog for WorkAI 1.0 launch and post-launch optimization.

## Priority experiment areas

1. Onboarding completion
2. First offer/request creation
3. First proposal
4. Funded deal
5. Released deal
6. Review rate
7. AI usage
8. Referral and invite behavior

## Experiment backlog

| # | Experiment | Hypothesis | Metric | Risk | Success threshold |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Onboarding step count | Fewer steps increase completion | Onboarding completion | Low | +10% completion |
| 2 | AI price button placement | More visible AI price increases usage | AI price usage | Low | +20% usage |
| 3 | Profile completeness prompt | In-app prompt improves profile quality | Profile quality | Low | +15% quality |
| 4 | Offer template | Templates reduce listing friction | Time to first offer | Low | -30% time |
| 5 | First deal incentive | A launch credit increases deal rate | Deal rate | Medium | +10% deals |
| 6 | Review timing | Immediate prompt increases review rate | Review rate | Low | +15% reviews |
| 7 | Welcome email | Email improves D7 retention | D7 retention | Low | +5% D7 |
| 8 | Push timing | Better timing increases opens | Open rate | Low | +10% opens |
| 9 | Trust display | More trust context improves proposal acceptance | Accept rate | Low | +5% accept rate |
| 10 | AI match default | Auto-match increases AI usage | Match usage | Low | +20% usage |
| 11 | Category-first listing | Category-first UI speeds listing creation | Time to listing | Low | -20% time |
| 12 | Social proof | Activity proof increases action rate | Action rate | Low | +5% action |
| 13 | Empty state CTA | Better empty state increases first action | First action rate | Low | +10% first action |
| 14 | Deal milestones | Milestones improve completion | Release rate | Medium | +10% releases |
| 15 | Invite flow | Easier invite flow increases referrals | Referral rate | Low | +20% referrals |

## Run rules

- Run one high-impact experiment per flow at a time.
- Do not experiment on trust or payment copy without manual review.
- Stop experiments that harm completion, trust, or dispute rate.
- Use launch cohorts to avoid mixing pre-launch and post-launch behavior.
