# WorkAI Launch Communication Plan

## Purpose

Launch communication templates and messaging for WorkAI 1.0.

## Positioning statement

WorkAI is an AI-powered work marketplace where people find the right help for projects faster through AI matching, trust scores, and structured deal workflows.

## FAQ copy

### What is WorkAI?

WorkAI is an AI-powered marketplace connecting people who need work done with people who can solve it.

### Is it free?

The 1.0 early launch can be free or limited depending on the active launch configuration.

### How does trust work?

Users build trust through completed deals, reviews, verification, and behavior on the platform.

### How does AI matching work?

AI compares requests, offers, skills, pricing, trust, and history to suggest better matches.

### How are disputes handled?

Disputes are reviewed by the operator/admin workflow with supporting context from deal, risk, and fraud signals.

## Trust and safety copy

```text
WorkAI is designed around trust:

- Trust scores
- Reviews
- Deal workflows
- Dispute handling
- Operator review for risky cases

Report issues in-app or through support.
```
